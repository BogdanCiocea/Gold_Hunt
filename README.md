
                                Gold Hunt
                                -the game-

 # Objective:
    * Collect as many gold as you can, there are no limits to the levels ... yet
    * Avoid the mines (they can be shot down with the lasers)
    * Check for easter eggs

 # Controls:

    * Moving:
        W - up
        A - left
        S - down
        D - walking
    
    * Shooting:
      4 - left
      8 - up
      6 - right
      2 - down
    
    * Playlist:
      9 - forward
      7 - backward

 # Have fun!
